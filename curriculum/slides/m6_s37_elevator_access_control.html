Elevator Control Architecture: Car vs Machine Room

  • Car Operating Panel (COP): RFID/Biometric reader mounted directly inside elevator cabin beside floor buttons.
  • Elevator Machine Room Controller: Multi-relay expansion board mounted in top elevator machine room.
  • Traveling Cable Harness: Transmits RS485/Wiegand data signals through flexible traveling cable running down elevator shaft.
Architecture Rule: Place relay controller in top machine room; mount RFID reader inside elevator car.
(37-3)

Floor Restrictor Relay Expansion Boards (16 / 32 Floors)

  • Dry Contact Relay Intercept: Relays wired in series with physical elevator button wiring to interrupt floor signals.
  • 16-Floor Relay Expansion Modules: Modular relay boards daisy-chained via RS485 to control up to 128 floors.
  • Normally-Open (NO) Button Release: Valid card tap energizes target floor relay for 5 seconds, enabling button press.
Wiring Standard: Floor relays intercept physical elevator button wires to enable button press only on valid card tap.
(37-4)

Traveling Cable Wiring & Shielded Noise Protection

  • Flex-Rated Traveling Cables: Ultra-flexible multi-conductor cables designed for millions of continuous bending cycles.
  • Heavy Motor EMI Interference: Elevator drive motors generate severe high-voltage electrical noise inside shaft.
  • Shielded Twisted Pair (STP): Mandatory STP cabling with single-point grounding to prevent reader data corruption.
Wiring Rule: ALWAYS use shielded twisted-pair (STP) cable inside elevator shafts to prevent motor EMI noise.

User Access Permission Mapping (Multi-Floor & Penthouse)

  • Single-Floor Tenant Access: Residential tenant card unlocks ONLY assigned apartment floor (e.g. Floor 14).
  • Executive Multi-Floor Access: Managers and maintenance staff cards unlock multiple corporate floors.
  • Direct Penthouse Call: Tapping VIP card automatically selects Penthouse button and dispatches elevator without button press.
VMS Software Rule: Access control software maps exact floor button permission groups to user RFID cards.

Fire Alarm & Emergency Bypass Overrides

  • Fire Alarm Dry Contact Input: Dedicated NC fire relay input on lift controller triggers instant system bypass.
  • 100% Floor Release on Fire: All floor restrictor relays drop out, making ALL floor buttons freely selectable for evacuation.
  • Key-Switch Manual Bypass: Physical key-switch in machine room allows elevator technicians to bypass access control completely.
Life Safety Rule: Fire alarm triggers MUST instantly drop out all floor relays to allow free elevator evacuation.

Destination Dispatch & Touchless Elevator Integration

  • Lobby Destination Kiosk: Passengers tap card at lobby turnstile; system assigns specific Elevator Car (e.g. Car C).
  • Zero Inside Button Presses: Elevator travels directly to assigned floor without pressing buttons inside cabin.
  • High-Speed Traffic Flow: Increases building elevator handling capacity by 30% in high-rise towers.
Commercial Trend: Destination dispatch turnstiles assign elevator cars at lobby turnstiles touchlessly.

Biometric & QR Code Elevator Readers

  • Face Recognition Cabin Readers: Compact 3D face terminals mounted in COP for hands-free floor selection.
  • Visitor QR Code Readers: Temporary QR codes sent to visitor phones allow 1-time elevator ride to host floor.
  • Vandal-Resistant Flush Mount: Flush-mounted stainless steel reader housings prevent passenger tampering.
Visitor Access: QR code readers allow temporary visitor floor access without issuing physical RFID cards.

Troubleshooting Elevator Relay & Communication Drops

  • Relay Click but No Button Light: Blown elevator button fuse or loose terminal connection on button wiring harness.
  • Intermittent Reader Drops: Cable fatigue or broken conductor inside traveling cable; switch to spare conductor pair.
  • RS485 Termination Resistor: Install 120-ohm termination resistor on end of long RS485 elevator lift controller bus.
Field Fix: If elevator reader drops intermittently, switch to a spare conductor pair inside the traveling cable.
