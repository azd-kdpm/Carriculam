  • Palm Vein & Iris: Ultra-secure internal vascular/iris pattern matching with zero FAR (False Acceptance Rate) for bio-labs.
Modality Choice: Specify touchless 3D Face Recognition for high-throughput corporate lobbies.

Troubleshooting Backlight Glare, Dirty Sensors & Registration Faults

  • Direct Sunlight Glare: Strong outdoor sunlight blinds IR cameras; install terminal sunhood canopy.
  • Failure To Enroll (FTE): Re-enroll users with glasses off, or adjust terminal mounting height to match eye level.
  • Dirty Optical Glass: Clean camera/sensor glass with microfiber cloth; avoid harsh chemical solvents.
Field Fix: Install sunhood canopies over outdoor facial terminals to prevent IR camera blinding from direct sunlight.
