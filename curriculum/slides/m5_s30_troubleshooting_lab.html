(31-3)

Ticket #101 Investigation: PoE Voltage Drop & CCA Cable

  • Symptom Analysis: Cameras operate normally during daylight but reboot continuously when IR LEDs activate at night.
  • PoE Tester Finding: Measured voltage drops from 48V DC down to 37V DC under 800mA IR LED load on 85m CCA cable.
  • Resolution Action: Replaced 85m CCA cable with 100% Solid Bare Copper CAT6 cable; voltage remains stable at 46V DC.
Resolution #101: Replacing CCA cable with Solid Bare Copper CAT6 eliminated night-time IR rebooting.
(31-4)

Ticket #102 Investigation: OTDR Fiber Break Distance Pinpointing

  • Symptom Analysis: Switch B SFP port link LED is unlit; 10GbE fiber backbone connection is completely severed.
  • OTDR Trace Result: OTDR trace indicates sharp non-reflective signal loss event at exactly 1,240 meters from patch panel.
  • Resolution Action: Technicians dispatched to 1,240m conduit vault, removed crushed cable section, and performed fusion splice (< 0.02dB loss).
Resolution #102: OTDR pinpointed fiber break at 1,240 meters; fusion splice restored 10GbE trunk link.
(31-5)

Ticket #103 Investigation: ARP Conflict & Duplicate IP

  • Symptom Analysis: Gate ANPR camera video feed flickers and drops every 15 minutes on NVR console.
  • ARP Table Inspection: Running "arp -a" revealed IP 192.168.1.100 flapping between camera MAC and office printer MAC.
  • Resolution Action: Re-assigned gate ANPR camera to dedicated static IP 10.20.1.50 on isolated CCTV VLAN 20.
Resolution #103: Re-assigning static IP to isolated VLAN 20 eliminated duplicate IP collision.
(31-6)

Wireshark Packet Capture Verification & Protocol Audit

  • SPAN Port Audit: Configured SPAN port mirror on core switch and captured 5-minute video stream payload.
  • RTSP Handshake Check: Verified clean RTSP/1.0 200 OK responses and TCP stream transport over Port 554.
  • Zero Packet Drop: Verified zero TCP retransmissions and 0% UDP frame loss across all 64 camera streams.
Audit Standard: Wireshark SPAN port packet capture confirms 100% clean RTSP streaming health.
