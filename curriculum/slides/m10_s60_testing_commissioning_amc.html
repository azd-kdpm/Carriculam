Pre-Commissioning Physical & Electrical Integrity Audit

  • Insulation Resistance Testing (500V Megger): Verify zero short circuits between phase, neutral, and earth conductors (> 100 MΩ).
  • PoE Voltage Load Inspection: Measure PoE voltage at distant camera RJ45 plugs (44V - 57V DC) under full infrared night-load draw.
  • Polymorphic Ground Resistance Check: Ensure earth ground resistance is < 5Ω across all outdoor camera poles and rack busbars.
Testing Rule: ALWAYS measure PoE voltage at distant camera plugs during IR night-mode when power draw is highest.
(62-3)

Functional Commissioning: Video, Analytics & Storage Audits

  • Camera Field of View & Focus Audit: Adjust motorized varifocal lenses for pin-sharp focus at target DORI distances.
  • AI Analytics Calibration: Verify line-crossing and ANPR trigger accuracy by walking across virtual tripwires.
  • RAID Failover & Retention Test: Pull out one hard drive from RAID 5 array to verify zero video data loss and auto-rebuild.
RAID Verification: Pulling a hard drive from a live RAID 5 NVR proves zero-loss fault tolerance to the client.

System Handover Documentation & Comprehensive As-Built Drawings

  • As-Built CAD Floorplans: Updated floorplan drawings reflecting actual camera locations, conduit runs, and rack placement.
  • System Configuration Matrix: Spreadsheet detailing IP addresses, MAC addresses, switch port numbers, and admin passwords.
  • User Operation & Administrator Manual: Easy 2-page cheat sheet for daily video playback, export, and password resets.
Handover Standard: Handover documentation package MUST include as-built drawings, IP credentials, and warranties.

Formal Client Sign-Off & Acceptance Certificate (FAC)

  • Provisional Acceptance Certificate (PAC): Signed upon 100% hardware installation and successful 72-hour burn-in period.
  • Snag List (Punch List) Clearance: Documenting minor cosmetic defects (e.g. 1 un-labeled wire) and fixing them within 7 days.
  • Final Acceptance Certificate (FAC): Triggers final 10% payment release and starts the 1-year warranty coverage clock.
Payment Release: Clearing the client Punch List within 7 days guarantees prompt 10% final payment release.

Comprehensive End-User Training & Operator Certification

  • System Administrator Training: Training IT managers on user permission levels, firmware updates, and RAID status monitoring.
  • Signed Training Attendance Log: Documenting client attendance to prevent future "we were never trained" disputes.
Training Best Practice: Obtain signed training logs from security staff to confirm successful knowledge transfer.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC): Comprehensive vs Non-Comprehensive

  • Comprehensive AMC (15% - 20% of Project Value): Covers quarterly preventive maintenance, emergency callouts, AND free spare parts replacement.
  • Non-Comprehensive AMC (8% - 12% of Project Value): Covers preventive maintenance and labor only; spare parts are billed separately to client.
  • Predictable Recurring Revenue: AMCs create steady annual recurring revenue (ARR) for your system integration business.
Business Strategy: AMC service contracts convert one-off project installs into multi-year recurring cash flow.
(62-8)

Quarterly Preventive Maintenance (PM) Checklist & Audit

  • Camera Lens & Housing Cleaning: Wipe camera dome glass with anti-static lens cleaner; check IP67 gasket seals for moisture.
  • HDD S.M.A.R.T Health Check: Inspect hard drive bad sectors and temperature logs; replace failing drives before total crash.
  • UPS Battery & Server Fan Audit: Test UPS battery backup runtime under load; clean dust filters in server rack fans.
Preventive Value: Quarterly S.M.A.R.T health checks catch failing NVR hard drives weeks before video data loss occurs.
(62-9)

Troubleshooting Intermittent Video Loss & False Alarm Spike Diagnostics

  • Intermittent Camera Dropping at Night: PoE switch power budget exceeded when camera IR LEDs switch ON at dusk; upgrade PoE switch.
  • NVR Rebooting Randomly: Overheated NVR CPU due to clogged fan filters or failing 12V DC power supply adapter.
  • Spike in Motion False Alarms: Spider webs across IR lens reflecting light; clean dome lens and apply anti-insect spray.
Field Diagnostics: Cameras dropping only at 06:00 PM indicates PoE switch power budget overload from IR night lights.
