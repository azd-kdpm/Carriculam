Leading-Edge vs Trailing-Edge Phase-Cut TRIAC Dimming

  • Leading-Edge (Forward Phase TRIAC): Cuts front of AC sine wave; designed for legacy incandescent and magnetic transformer loads.
  • Trailing-Edge (Reverse Phase MOSFET): Cuts back of AC sine wave; essential for dimmable LED driver bulbs to prevent buzzing.
  • Minimum Load Requirement: Requires minimum 5W load or dummy load resistor to prevent LED flicker at 10% brightness.
Dimming Standard: Trailing-edge MOSFET dimmers eliminate annoying transformer buzzing in dimmable LED downlights.
(55-3)

0-10V Analog vs DALI Digital Lighting Control

  • 0-10V Analog Control (2-Wire Polarity): 10V DC represents 100% brightness; 1V DC represents 10% dimming; simple non-addressable wiring.
  • DALI-2 Digital Addressable Interface: 2-wire unpolarized 16V bus addressing up to 64 individual light fixtures per gateway.
  • Smooth Logarithmic Dimming Curve: Matches human eye perception for natural ultra-smooth dimming down to 0.1%.
Commercial Lighting: DALI-2 digital buses allow individual addressable control of 64 fixtures on a single 2-wire loop.

Motorized Curtain Tracks & Roller Blinds Wiring

  • AC 230V 4-Wire Motors: Earth + Neutral + Live Open + Live Close wires connected to dual-relay curtain switch module.
  • Dry Contact Pulse Motors: Low-voltage pulse control for integration with automation controllers and smart switches.
  • Manual Touch-Motion Release: Gently pulling curtain fabric by hand triggers motor to open/close automatically.
Wiring Rule: Mechanical interlock on curtain relays prevents simultaneous OPEN and CLOSE live voltage feed.

Travel Calibration & Percentage Position Memory

App Precision: Travel time calibration enables slider controls for precise 50% open positioning.

Circadian Rhythm Lighting & Tunable White (CCT)

  • Correlated Color Temperature (CCT): Shifts LED color temperature from 6500K Cool White (day) to 2700K Warm White (night).
  • Automated Solar Sun Sync: Automatically adjusts light color temperature based on local outdoor sun elevation angles.
  • Melatonin Sleep Health: Warm 2700K lighting in the evening boosts melatonin production for healthier sleep patterns.
Wellness Design: Automated circadian lighting shifts CCT smoothly to match natural sunlight progression.

Troubleshooting LED Buzzing, Ghosting & Motor Reversals

  • LED Buzzing / Humming: Replace leading-edge TRIAC dimmer with trailing-edge MOSFET dimmer module.
  • LED Ghosting / Glowing Off: Install 0.1μF 275V AC dummy load resistor across load terminals to drain leakage current.
  • Curtain Motor Operates Backwards: Reverse Open/Close wire terminals or toggle motor direction setting in app.
Field Troubleshooting: LED ghosting when turned off is solved 100% by adding a 0.1uF dummy load resistor.
