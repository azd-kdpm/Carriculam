Thermal Imaging Principles & Microbolometer Sensors

  • Uncooled VOx Microbolometer: Vanadium Oxide sensor measures heat radiation emitted by objects instead of reflected light.

  • Light-Independent Detection: Operates in 100% total darkness, thick smoke, heavy fog, and blinding glare.
Physics Principle: Thermal cameras see heat energy emitted by objects, NOT light reflected off them.
