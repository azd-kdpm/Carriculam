High-Speed Shutter Tuning (1/1000s to 1/2000s)

  • Freeze Fast Motion: Set shutter speed between 1/1000s and 1/2000s to eliminate vehicle motion blur.
  • Headlight Glare Suppression: Fast shutter prevents oncoming vehicle headlights from blinding the sensor.
  • Traffic Lanes: Captures vehicles traveling at speeds up to 160 km/h on highway lanes.
Shutter Rule: Set 1/1000s shutter speed for parking gates; 1/2000s for highway traffic.

OCR Accuracy Factors & Degradation Causes

  • Clean vs Dirty Plates: Muddy or covered plates degrade OCR recognition confidence from 99% down to 60%.

  • Extreme Weather: Heavy rain, snow, or fog attenuates infrared light reflection.
Accuracy Standard: Train localized OCR neural networks to handle country-specific plate fonts.
