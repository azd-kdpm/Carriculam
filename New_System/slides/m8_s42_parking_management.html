UHF Long-Range RFID (860-960MHz) vs Passive Proximity

  • UHF EPC Gen2 Passive Tags: 6-12 meter reading range mounted on vehicle windshield or headlamp.
  • Hands-Free Drive-Through: Vehicles enter without driver rolling down window or tapping card.
  • Anti-Tamper Destructive Tags: Windshield sticker breaks internally if peeled off to prevent tag sharing between cars.
Gated Communities: UHF RFID windshield tags deliver hands-free 10-meter gate opening for residents.

Deep Learning ANPR License Plate Recognition Cameras

  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR): Embedded AI neural engine extracts plate numbers in < 50ms with 98.5% accuracy.
  • Dual-Shutter High-Speed WDR: Captures reflective license plates in pitch darkness using IR strobe illuminator.
  • White List Database Matching: Opens boom barrier automatically for registered VIPs and monthly subscriber vehicles.
ANPR Accuracy: Integrated IR strobe illuminators eliminate headlight glare and read plates in pitch dark.

Parking Ticket Dispensers & Barcode / QR Thermal Kiosks

  • Entry Ticket Dispenser: Visitor presses button over presence loop; dispenses 2D QR code ticket with entry timestamp.
  • Barcode / QR Scanner at Exit: Scans paid ticket at exit gate; verifies payment status before releasing barrier arm.
  • Low Ticket Roll Alert: Sends automated SNMP alert to parking control room when paper roll is 90% depleted.
Commercial Parking: Entry dispensers issue 2D QR tickets only when a vehicle is physically present over the loop.

Variable Message Displays (VMD) & Real-Time Bay Counting

  • Full / Available Spot Counters: Real-time LED screen at entrance displays available parking spots per floor (e.g. B1: 42 FREE).
  • Ultrasonic Overhead Space Sensors: Green/Red LED ceiling sensor above each bay indicates spot occupancy in real time.
  • Full Lot Interlock: Automatically locks entry barrier when total available parking spots reach zero.
Driver Guidance: Overhead Green/Red ultrasonic sensors guide drivers directly to open parking bays.

Anti-Passback & Vehicle Audit Log Databases

  • Vehicle Anti-Passback (APB): UHF tag or ANPR plate cannot be reused for entry until it has logged a valid exit punch.
  • High-Resolution Entry/Exit Snapshots: Stores dual high-res photos (plate + driver face) for every transaction.
  • Revenue Reconciliation Reports: Generates daily cash, card, and subscriber revenue reports for parking operators.
Audit Integrity: Dual photo snapshots (plate + car) eliminate revenue leakage and ticket swapping fraud.

EV Charging Station Interlocks & Payment Gateway Integration

  • Combined Parking + Charging Bill: Merges hourly parking tariff and kWh electricity consumption into single checkout bill.
  • Idle Fee Penalty: Charges penalty fee if fully charged EV stays parked in charging bay longer than 30 minutes.
EV Optimization: Idle fee penalties encourage EV drivers to move vehicles once charging completes.

Troubleshooting ANPR Plate Misreads & UHF Tag Interference

  • ANPR Reading Dirty / Curved Plates: Adjust camera optical zoom, shutter speed (1/1000s), and IR illuminator angle.
  • UHF Tag Range Drops < 2 Meters: Metallic windshield tinting (UV film) blocks UHF signals; use headlamp or license plate RFID tags.
  • Multiple Tags Detected Simultaneously: Adjust UHF reader RF power output (e.g. 26dBm to 20dBm) to restrict reading zone.
Field Troubleshooting: UV metallic windshield tinting blocks UHF signals; switch to headlamp-mounted RFID tags.
