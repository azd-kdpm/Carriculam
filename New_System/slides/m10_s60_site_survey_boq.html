(60-2)

Physical Site Survey Checklist & Field Measurements

  • Camera Field of View (FoV) Measurements: Measure lens focal length (2.8mm, 4mm, 6mm, 12mm) to match desired DORI distance.
  • Illumination & Backlight Audit: Identify strong background sunlight (requiring True WDR 120dB) and zero-lux dark spots.
  • Power & Network Infrastructure Audit: Locating main DB panels, existing conduit pathways, network rack spaces, and ground earthing points.
Survey Rule: Never quote a CCTV job without physically measuring cable run lengths and verifying WDR lighting needs.
(60-3)

Bill of Quantities (BOQ) Structuring & Itemized Component Breakdowns

  • Hardware Core Items: IP Cameras, NVR/DVR, Hard Drives, PoE Switches, and Server Racks itemized with exact model numbers.
  • Cabling & Containment Consumables: Cat6 UTP drums, PVC conduits, metal trunking, RJ45 connectors, and patch cords (+15% extra allowance).
  • Labour & Professional Services: Separate lines for civil trenching, cable pulling, rack termination, camera mounting, and ETS/NVR commissioning.
BOQ Precision: Always add a 15% wastage margin to cable drum estimates for vertical drops and conduit bends.
(60-4)

Cable Run Distance Math, Voltage Drop & Fiber Optic Triggers

  • Ethernet Distance Limit (90m / 300ft): Solid copper Cat6 cable max length for PoE data + power transmission.
  • PoE Extenders / Repeaters: Required if cable run exceeds 90m up to 200m; consumes 2W PoE power.
  • Fiber Optic Upgrade Trigger (> 100m): Mandatory shift to Single-Mode Fiber Optic cable (10G SFP+) for long perimeter distances up to 10km.
Engineering Standard: Cable runs beyond 90 meters MUST use Fiber Optic cables or PoE Extender repeaters.
(60-5)

Professional Quotation Pricing, Margins & Cost-Plus Strategy

  • Cost-Plus Pricing Formula: Total Quotation Price = (Hardware Cost + Labour Cost + Overheads) x (1 + Profit Margin %).
  • Healthy Profit Margin Standard: 25% - 35% margin on hardware equipment; 50% margin on installation labour services.
  • Contingency Fund (5%): Protects against unexpected wall drilling obstacles or scope changes during installation.
Business Math: Maintain a minimum 30% gross profit margin to cover business overheads, warranty calls, and growth.
(60-6)

Terms of Payment, Payment Milestones & Risk Mitigation

  • 50-40-10 Payment Structure: 50% Advance upon contract signing; 40% Delivery of materials on site; 10% Final testing & handoff.
  • Material Ownership Retention: Contract clause retaining vendor ownership of equipment until 100% final payment is cleared.
  • Scope Creep Change Orders: Formal Change Request Form required for any extra cameras or wiring added mid-project.
Financial Security: Never deliver hardware to client sites without receiving the 50% advance payment first.
(60-7)

Formal Commercial Proposal & Technical SLA Documenting

  • Executive Summary & Scope of Work: Clear high-level summary outlining client pain points and proposed ELV solutions.
  • Service Level Agreement (SLA): Guarantees 4-hour response time for critical NVR/CCTV outages during 1-year warranty period.
  • Manufacturer Warranty Terms: Highlights 3-year replacement warranty on cameras and NVR hard drives.
Client Trust: Clear SLAs and 3-year warranty commitments win major commercial enterprise contracts.
(60-8)

Vendor Comparison & Tender Bid Preparation

  • Commercial Tender Documents: Responding to formal RFPs (Request for Proposal) for government or bank projects.
  • Compliance Matrix Table: Side-by-side technical compliance table proving hardware meets every single RFP specification.
  • Trade Discount Sourcing: Leveraging distributor volume pricing (Hikvision/Dahua Gold Partner discounts) to undercut competitors.
Tender Winning Strategy: Use distributor volume discounts to submit lower prices while keeping higher profit margins.
(60-9)

Troubleshooting Quotation Under-estimation & Material Shortages

  • Under-estimated Cable Quantities: Wall obstacles forced 50m longer cable runs; solved by 15% BOQ contingency buffer.
  • Client Unreasonable Scope Additions: Client requests 2 extra cameras for free; submit formal Change Order with itemized cost.
  • Delayed Hardware Shipments: Maintain buffer stock of popular 4MP IP cameras and 8-port PoE switches in workshop.
Field Protection: Handle scope changes with formal written Change Orders to avoid working at a financial loss.
