Switched-Mode Power Supply (SMPS) Principles

  • High Efficiency (85%+): SMPS switches power at high frequencies (50kHz+) reducing energy loss and heat.
  • Compact Size: Uses smaller high-frequency transformers compared to bulky linear transformers.
  • Multi-Output Channels: Provides separate fused 12V DC terminals for multi-camera CCTV installations.
Advantage: SMPS units deliver stable 12V DC power with minimal heat and compact footprint.
(5-3)

Linear vs SMPS Power Supplies Comparison

  • Linear PSU: Uses heavy 50Hz iron transformer; low noise output but low efficiency (< 50%) and high heat.
  • SMPS PSU: High efficiency (85%+), lightweight, wide input voltage range (90V-260V AC).
  • ELV Application: Use SMPS for CCTV/Access control panels; use linear PSU for ultra-sensitive audio.
Comparison: SMPS handles wide AC voltage fluctuations (90V - 260V AC) without overheating.

Power Supply Sizing & Load Calculations

  • Total Load Calculation: Add up wattage of all cameras, IR illuminators, and access controllers.
  • 20% Safety Derating Margin: Size PSU to operate at maximum 80% capacity for long service life.
  • Inrush Current: Account for high initial power spikes when IR LEDs turn on at night.

Voltage Regulation & Potentiometer Adjustment

  • V-ADJ Trimmer Pot: Built-in potentiometer allows boosting 12V DC output up to 13.8V DC.
  • Line Compensation: Boost voltage slightly to compensate for long cable distance voltage drops.
  • Tight Regulation (Â±1%): Keeps output voltage steady even when camera loads switch on and off.
Technician Tip: Adjust V-ADJ to 13.5V to offset long cable voltage drops on distant cameras.
(5-6)

Multi-Channel Fused Power Distribution Boards

  • PTC Auto-Reset Fuses: Polymer fuses automatically reset after a camera cable short circuit is cleared.
  • Individual Channel LED: Red LED turns off if a specific camera fuse blows, speeding up diagnostics.
  • Short Circuit Isolation: Prevents a short circuit on one camera from shutting down the entire CCTV system.
Design Standard: Use PTC auto-reset fused power distribution boxes for multi-camera CCTV.
(5-7)

Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) Technology

  • Online Double-Conversion: Zero switchover transfer time, protecting NVRs against power outages.
  • SLA Battery Charger: Integrated float charger maintains 12V Sealed Lead-Acid batteries safely.
  • Autonomy Calculation: Size battery Amp-Hours (Ah) to provide required backup runtime (e.g. 4 hours).
Best Practice: Use online UPS units for NVR storage servers to prevent hard drive corruption.
(5-8)

SLA Battery Charging & Maintenance

  • Float Charge Voltage: Maintain SLA battery float charge at 13.6V - 13.8V DC.
  • Deep Discharge Protection: Disconnect battery when voltage drops below 10.5V to prevent cell damage.
  • Battery Lifespan: Replace SLA batteries every 2 to 3 years in security power supply panels.
Maintenance Standard: Replace SLA backup batteries every 24-36 months.
