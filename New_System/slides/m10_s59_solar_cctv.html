Solar Panel Sizing: Peak Sun Hours (PSH) & Daily Watt-Hour (Wh) Calculations

  • Daily Load Calculation (Wh): A 10W 4G IP Camera running 24 hours draws 10W x 24h = 240Wh daily energy.
  • Peak Sun Hours (PSH = 4.5h): Solar panel power output calculated as Total Load / PSH x 1.3 System Derating.
  • Minimum Solar Panel Wattage: 240Wh / 4.5h x 1.3 = 69.3W → Select 80W Monocrystalline PV Panel.
Engineering Rule: Always add a 30% system derating safety factor to account for dust, heat, and wire losses.
(59-3)

LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) vs AGM / Gel Deep-Cycle Batteries

  • LiFePO4 Lithium Advantage: 3,500+ deep discharge cycles (80% DoD) vs 500 cycles for lead-acid AGM batteries.
  • Autonomy Days Sizing (3 Days): Sized for 3 consecutive rainy/cloudy days: 240Wh x 3 days = 720Wh total storage.
  • Amp-Hour Rating (12V): 720Wh / 12.8V = 56.25Ah → Select 12.8V 60Ah LiFePO4 Battery Pack.
Battery Choice: LiFePO4 batteries last 10+ years outdoors and withstand deep 80% discharges without degradation.

Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) vs PWM Charge Controllers

  • MPPT Controller (98% Efficiency): Dynamically adjusts voltage/current to extract maximum solar wattage in low light or high heat.
  • PWM Controller (70% Efficiency): Clamps panel voltage directly to battery voltage, wasting 30% of available solar power.
  • Load Terminal Low-Voltage Cutoff (LVD): Disconnects camera load automatically if battery drops below 11.1V to prevent battery damage.
Efficiency Mandate: MPPT controllers yield 30% more energy from solar panels during overcast winter days.

4G/5G Cellular Routers, Industrial SIMs & Data Bandwidth Optimization

  • Industrial LTE Routers (Teltonika / Sunell): Dual-SIM failover routers operating on 12V DC power directly.
  • Sub-Stream Bandwidth Saving: Stream 256kbps H.265 sub-stream for live viewing; save 4K high-res video to local SD card.
  • Monthly Data Math: Motion-only cloud upload consumes < 5GB/month data vs 300GB/month for 24/7 continuous streaming.
Bandwidth Rule: Transmit low-res sub-streams over 4G and record 4K video locally to keep SIM data under 5GB/month.

Solar Tilt Angle & Geographic Sun Azimuth Optimization

  • Optimal Solar Panel Tilt Angle: Set panel tilt angle equal to local geographic latitude + 15° for maximum winter sun harvest.
  • True South Orientation (Northern Hemisphere): Point solar panels directly towards True South (using compass declination offset).
  • Anti-Vandal High Pole Mounting: Mount solar panel at 4m - 6m pole height to prevent shade obstruction and theft.
Orientation Rule: In the Northern Hemisphere, point panels to True South at (Latitude + 15 degrees) for winter optimization.
(59-7)

Weatherproof IP67 Enclosures, Thermal Management & Lightning Protection

  • IP67 NEMA 4X Enclosure: Gasket-sealed aluminum/polycarbonate outdoor cabinet housing battery and MPPT controller.
  • Thermostatic Cooling Fan / Heatsink: Vents enclosure if internal temperature exceeds 45°C; insulates battery in winter.
  • DC Surge Protection Device (SPD): 10kA DC SPD protects MPPT controller and camera inputs against lightning surges.
Protection Rule: Ground the solar pole frame with a copper earthing rod (< 5 ohms) to divert lightning strikes.
(59-8)

Low-Power PIR Wake-Up Cameras vs Continuous Recording

  • Ultra-Low Power Standby (15mW): Camera stays in deep sleep; PIR sensor wakes camera in 0.2s upon detecting human motion.
  • Continuous Commercial Recording (3W - 8W): Mandatory for perimeter security; requires 100W+ solar panel and 100Ah battery.
  • Radar + PIR Dual Detection: 24GHz mmWave radar combined with PIR eliminates false wakes from blowing tree branches.
Power Modes: PIR wake-up cameras use 95% less solar power than continuous recording cameras.
(59-9)

Troubleshooting Battery Depletion, MPPT Errors & 4G Signal Drops

  • Battery Dead After 2 Days: Solar panel shaded by tree branches or panel tilt angle incorrect; prune trees and check Voc.
  • MPPT Error Code (Overvoltage): PV panel series voltage exceeds controller max VOC limit; rewire panels in parallel.
  • 4G Offline in Remote Locations: Low RSRP signal (< -110dBm); install high-gain 9dBi directional Yagi LTE antenna on pole.
Field Fix: If 4G signal is weak (-110dBm), add a 9dBi directional Yagi LTE antenna aimed at the nearest cell tower.
