Optical Zoom vs Digital Zoom (25x / 42x Math)

  • True Optical Zoom: Motorized glass element movement changes focal length (e.g. 4.8mm to 120mm = 25x Optical Zoom).
  • Zero Resolution Loss: Retains 100% full 4K sensor pixel resolution across the entire zoom range.
  • Digital Zoom Pixelation: Simply crops and enlarges existing pixels, resulting in blurry pixelated images.
Sizing Rule: ALWAYS rely on Optical Zoom rating for long-distance facial identification over 150 meters.
