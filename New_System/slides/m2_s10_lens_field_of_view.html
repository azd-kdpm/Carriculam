Dome Cameras (Vandal-Resistant & Discrete)

  • Discrete Aesthetics: Compact dome shape blends into indoor commercial ceilings and hotel lobbies.
  • Vandal Resistance: Polycarbonate clear dome cover protects lens against physical strikes and tampering.
  • 3-Axis Adjustment: Internal gimbal allows ceiling, wall, or inclined surface mounting flexibility.
Application: Use dome cameras in building entrances, elevators, and public corridors.
(10-3)

Bullet Cameras (Outdoor Deterrence & Long IR)

  • Visual Deterrence: Prominent cylindrical shape discourages criminal activity in outdoor perimeters.
  • Integrated Sunshield: Adjustable top visor blocks direct sunlight glare and raindrops from lens face.
  • High-Power Long IR: Accommodates larger IR LED arrays reaching 60m to 100m night vision ranges.
Application: Ideal for outdoor building perimeters, parking lots, and fence lines.
(10-4)

Turret / Eyeball Cameras (Easy Aiming & Zero IR Bounce)

  • Zero IR Reflection: Lens and IR LEDs are physically separated, eliminating IR bounce-back fogging.
  • Ball-and-Socket Joint: Quick 3-axis ball rotation speeds up installer aiming without disassembling housing.
  • Technician Favorite: Combines bullet performance with dome's compact ceiling footprint.
Installer Choice: Eyeball cameras eliminate IR glass reflection and drastically reduce installation time.

Ingress Protection (IP66, IP67, IP68 Weatherproofing)

  • IP66 Dust & Water Jet: Complete protection against dust ingress and powerful heavy rain water jets.
  • IP67 Temporary Immersion: Withstands 30 minutes underwater immersion at 1 meter depth.
  • Gland Seals & O-Rings: Always tighten rubber cable gland seals to maintain factory IP weather rating.
Weather Standard: Require minimum IP66 rating for all outdoor camera enclosures.
(10-7)

Impact Protection Ratings (IK08, IK10 Vandal Proof)

  • IK10 Rating (20 Joules): Protects against 5kg steel mass dropped from 40cm height (sledgehammer impact).
  • Cast Aluminum Enclosures: Heavy metal housing body prevents physical crushing or dislodging.
  • Security Torx Screws: Tamper-proof Star Torx screws prevent unauthorized opening of camera base.
Security Code: Install IK10 vandal-rated cameras in prisons, schools, and transit hubs.

Wall & Ceiling Brackets (Pendant & Gooseneck)

  • Gooseneck Wall Brackets: Extends cameras away from building walls to prevent roof eave view obstruction.
  • Pendant Ceiling Mounts: Suspends PTZ speed domes down from high warehouse or atrium ceilings.
  • Internal Cable Pass-Through: Conceals BNC and power cables inside bracket arm, protecting against wire cutting.
Vandal Protection: Use concealed cable pass-through brackets to prevent wire vandal cutting.
