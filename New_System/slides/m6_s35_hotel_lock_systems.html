Mifare 13.56MHz S50/S70 RFID Smart Card Standards

  • ISO 14443A Mifare Standard: High-frequency 13.56MHz encrypted RFID contactless smart cards.
  • Sector Encryption (Crypto1): Encrypts room number, check-in date, and check-out timestamp into card sectors.
  • Zero Contact Wear: Contactless tapping prevents mechanical card reader slot wear and liquid damage.
Card Technology: Mifare 13.56MHz RFID cards store check-in/out dates encrypted directly inside card sectors.

  • USB RFID Card Encoder: Writes guest access data onto Mifare keycard in under 1 second during check-in.
  • PMS API Integration: Integrates with Opera, Fidelio, and cloud PMS platforms via DLL/TCP socket interfaces.
Front Desk Workflow: Keycard encoder automatically programs room number and stay duration from PMS software.

Keycard Access Hierarchies (Guest, Maid, Master)

  • Guest Card: Unlocks assigned room only during valid check-in/out dates; automatically expires at noon on check-out day.
  • Maid Floor Card: Opens all rooms on assigned floor (e.g. 3rd Floor) during shift hours (8 AM - 4 PM).
  • Grand Master Card: Opens every hotel door 24/7, including double-locked deadbolted rooms during emergencies.
Security Hierarchy: Grand Master cards override internal deadbolts for emergency staff entry.

Keycard Energy Saver Switches & Circuit Control

  • Mifare Smart Energy Switch: Reads RFID card room number to ensure only valid guest keycards activate room power.
  • 30-Second Departure Delay: Retains lighting power for 30 seconds after card removal so guest can exit safely.
  • Energy Cost Savings: Cuts hotel electricity bills by 30% by shutting off AC and lights in unoccupied rooms.
Energy Efficiency: Smart RFID energy switches ensure paper business cards cannot trick room power circuits.

Forensic Proof: Handheld data collectors extract exact keycard entry logs to resolve guest theft claims.

Troubleshooting Card Read Failures & Expiry Errors

  • Card Red Light Beep Error: 3 red beeps indicates card has expired or room clock time is out of sync.
  • Lock Real-Time Clock Sync: Use Handheld Unit (HHD) to update lock internal clock time after battery replacement.
  • Demagnetized / Damaged Cards: Replace scratched Mifare cards; check RFID antenna coil solder joints inside lock.
Troubleshooting Tip: 3 red beeps on keycard tap means the lock internal clock needs time synchronization via HHD.
