High-Speed Brushless DC Motors vs AC Induction Drives

  • 24V / 36V Brushless DC (BLDC) Motors: Ultra-smooth operation with 100% continuous duty cycle for toll gates and high-traffic malls.
  • Variable Speed Control (1.5s to 6s): Fast 1.5-second opening for highway toll plazas; 3-6 second opening for commercial parking lots.
  • Zero Motor Wear & Overheating: BLDC gearboxes generate negligible heat compared to legacy AC induction motors.
Motor Selection: BLDC 24V motors operate continuously without thermal overload shutdowns in heavy toll plaza traffic.
(46-3)

Counterbalance Tension Spring Sizing & Balancing

  • Mechanical Weight Compensation: Heavy-duty steel springs counteract aluminum boom arm weight to reduce motor strain.
  • Spring Sizing Table: Select spring gauge (e.g. 5.5mm vs 6.5mm) based on boom arm length (3m, 4.5m, 6m).
  • 45-Degree Balance Test: Properly balanced boom arm remains stationary when manually disengaged at 45 degrees.
Installation Rule: ALWAYS perform the 45-degree balance test to prevent gearbox teeth stripping.
(46-4)

Boom Arm Profiles: Straight, Articulated & Fence Type

  • Octagonal Aluminum Straight Arm (3m - 6m): Lightweight aerodynamic profile with rubber anti-collision strip and LED light strip.
  • 90° / 180° Articulated Folding Arm: Folds horizontally for low-ceiling underground basement parking garages.
  • Fence Type Skirt Arm: Prevents pedestrians and animals from crawling under or stepping over barrier arm.
Garages Standard: Specify 90° articulated folding boom arms for underground parking ceilings under 2.4 meters.
(46-5)

Anti-Hit Safety: Rebound Sensors & Rubber Safety Strips

  • Auto-Reverse Impact Sensor: Motor controller senses mechanical resistance on descent and reverses arm in 0.1s.
  • Pneumatic / Pressure Safety Edges: Soft rubber bottom strip compresses upon hitting vehicle roof, instantly signaling arm to open.
  • Infrared Safety Photocells: Transmitter/Receiver beam mounted 50cm above ground stops arm from descending while vehicle breaks beam.
Vehicle Protection: Auto-reversing impact sensors prevent vehicle roof denting during accidental barrier drops.
(46-6)

Manual Release Clutch & Emergency Power Fail Operation

  • Rotary Key Clutch Release: Disengages gearbox mechanically allowing security guard to lift arm manually during blackouts.
  • Integrated 24V Battery Backup: Internal 24V 7Ah lead-acid batteries support 300+ continuous operations during power outages.
  • Auto-Open on Power Loss: Configurable Dip-switch setting forces barrier arm to open automatically upon main power failure.
Emergency Rule: Configure auto-open on power failure for fire evacuation vehicle routes.
(46-7)

Barrier Controller Motherboard & Limit Switch Tuning

  • Optical Hall-Effect Encoders: Digital motor encoder tracks exact arm angle for smooth deceleration buffer zone.
  • Opening / Closing Limit Adjustment: Fine-tunes horizontal 0° and vertical 90° stop points to prevent mechanical slamming.
  • Push-Button Terminal Inputs: Dry contact inputs for Open, Close, Stop, Safety Loop, and Fire Alarm release.
Tuning Tip: Adjust Hall-effect encoder deceleration buffers to eliminate noisy arm bouncing at 90 degrees.
(46-8)

Traffic Signal Lights & LED Boom Strip Integration

  • Red / Green LED Traffic Light: Mounted on barrier housing; Red signals stop, Green signals safe vehicle entry.
  • Flowing LED Boom Strip: LED strip along aluminum arm illuminates Red on descent and Green on ascent for night visibility.
  • Relay Interlocking: Barrier motherboard triggers traffic light relay contacts automatically based on arm position.
Night Safety: Flowing LED boom strips make barrier arms clearly visible to drivers in dark parking lots.
(46-9)

Troubleshooting Arm Slamming, Spring Snap & Motor Heat

  • Arm Slamming at 0° / 90°: Limit switch misaligned or deceleration buffer pot turned too low on motherboard.
  • Snapping Counterbalance Springs: Incorrect spring tension gauge chosen for heavy 6-meter boom arm.
  • Barrier Stalls Halfway: Disengage clutch and perform 45-degree balance test; adjust spring tensioning bolt.
Field Troubleshooting: Halfway barrier stalling is 95% caused by un-balanced spring tensioning.
