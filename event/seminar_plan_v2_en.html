Seminar Execution Plan v2

Careers of 2030 in Smart Automation & Security

1. Overview & Setup

This plan is tailored for a 2-hour promotional seminar starting at 10:30 AM. The technical display is designed around a specific, high-impact set of local hardware tools to demonstrate system integration capability.

On-Site Hardware Inventory

  • Android Smart Board Panel: Central stage hub displaying presentations, NVR video stream, and home automation dashboard.
  • NVR & IP Security Cameras: Live camera feeds with virtual tripwire zone alarms.
  • Home Automation Switch: Linked to a light bulb, fan, and motorized curtain.
  • Google Assistant Speaker: Local voice control interface.
  • Biometric Attendance Device: Used at entry for check-in demo.
  • Doorbell: Entrance visitor announcement demo.
  • Tools & Wires: Cat6 RJ45 punch-down tools, wire strippers, and network patch cables.

3. Stage Demo Guidelines

  • Step A (Smart Board): Touch the dashboard to toggle the lights and fan. Slide the curtain.
  • Step B (Voice): Command Google Assistant: "Hey Google, open the curtain" or "Hey Google, turn on the fan".
  • Step C (CCTV Security): Show the NVR feed. Walk in front of the camera to trigger the tripwire warning live.
  • Step D (Cabling): Hand out patch cables and show the punch-down tools to emphasize practical lab classes.
