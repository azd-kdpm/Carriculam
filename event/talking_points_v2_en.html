Presenter Talking Points Guide v2

Detailed Agenda & Stage Scripts

Welcome & Introduction of BotGi (11:00 AM - 11:08 AM)

  • Welcome all students and parents. Thank them for arriving early to settle in from the rain/local travel.
  • Introduce BotGi Tech Learning School: We are not just a training center. We are a skill development platform built to address the technological gap between traditional college degrees and industry demands.
  • Our focus is 100% practical, hands-on labs in Low-Voltage Engineering, IP Security, Smart Building Systems, and Home Automation.
Stage Action: Point to the custom dark purple portal landing page displayed on the Android Smart Board Panel.

Tutor & Student Introductions (11:08 AM - 11:15 AM)

  • Tutor Introduction: Share the lead tutor's academic credentials and years of physical field experience installing smart security and automation setups.
  • Student Introduction: Ask each student to stand up individually, introduce their name, educational background, and areas they are curious about (e.g. CCTV, Smart Locks, KNX).
  • This builds a comfortable class-like rapport and makes the seminar interactive.

Slide 1 to 7: Career Scope & Reality check (11:15 AM - 11:40 AM)

  • Slide 2 (Education Reality): College degrees are failing to secure jobs because of a lack of practical skill sets.
  • Slide 3 & 4 (Why Automation & Market Size): Automation market in India is expanding from ₹40,000 Cr to over ₹5 Lakh Crore by 2030. Every new building now requires smart switches, cameras, and fire protection.
  • Slide 5 (Kerala Growth): Local Kerala villas, hospitals, and apartments are actively integrating smart hardware. Splicers and integrators are highly sought after.
  • Slide 6 (What is BotGi): Explain our 5-Stage path: from skilled technicians to professional integrators, freelancers, and entrepreneurs.

Slide 8 to 16: Tech Modules & Live Demos (11:40 AM - 12:15 PM)

  • Slide 8 (Networking): Explain that network cabling is the foundation. Show Slide 8. *(Stage Action: Toggle lighting controls via the Smart Board touchscreen)*.
  • Slide 9 (CCTV & AI): Discuss facial recognition and plate cameras. *(Stage Action: Show live IP camera feeds from NVR and trigger virtual line crossing alerts)*.
  • Slide 11 (VDP & Access Control): Explain biometric readers and visitor logs. *(Stage Action: Point to the Attendance Device checked in during registration and ring the Smart Doorbell)*.
  • Slide 12 (Home Automation): Detail KNX wiring and Zigbee mesh networks. *(Stage Action: Issue voice commands to Google Assistant speaker: "Hey Google, turn on the fan" or "Hey Google, open the curtain" to trigger live devices)*.
  • Slide 13-16 (Solar, PA, Alarm, Fire): Highlight solar camera backups, 100V PA networks, burglar sensor loops, and modern Ajax fire panels.
Lab Showcase: Pick up Cat6 cables, punch-down tools, and crimpers. Show the parents that students learn by physically terminating, wiring, and testing these systems.

Slide 17: Special Offer & Q&A (12:15 PM - 12:30 PM)

  • Open the floor for questions from parents regarding course duration, fee installments, and certifications.
  • The BotGi Guarantee: Reassure parents that we guarantee practical skill sets that prepare students to be self-sufficient freelancers and entrepreneurs.
  • Special Announcement: Announce that the first 10 students to complete admission spot-registrations today receive a special seminar discount.
