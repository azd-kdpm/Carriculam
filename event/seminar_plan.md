## 3. Step-by-Step Live Stage Demonstration Flow (45 Mins)

This segment is designed to wow students and reassure parents through live technical competence.

### Step A: Structured Networking & Smart Board Control (10 Mins)
*   Show Slide 8 (Networking). Point out that all devices on stage are connected to a single local IP network.
*   Bring up the Home Automation app/dashboard on the **Android Smart Board Panel**.
*   Tap the screen to turn the light bulb on and off, turn the fan on, and slide the motorized curtain open/closed.

### Step B: Voice Automation Showcase (10 Mins)
*   Show Slide 12 (Home Automation). Introduce the concept of voice-assistant integration.
*   Issue live voice commands to the **Google Assistant Device**:
    *   *"Hey Google, turn on the fan."* (Fan starts spinning on stage)
    *   *"Hey Google, close the curtain."* (Motorized curtain slides shut)
    *   *"Hey Google, dim the lights."* (Light bulb dims)

### Step C: CCTV AI & Security Integration (15 Mins)
*   Show Slide 9 (CCTV & AI). Switch the Smart Board view to display the live feed from the **IP Cameras** connected to the **NVR**.
*   Trigger a live tripwire: Walk across the camera path to show how the NVR instantly draws a red box around you on the Smart Board and displays a notification.

### Step D: Hardware & Tool Demonstration (10 Mins)
*   Pick up the network crimper, punch-down tool, and patch cable.
*   Explain to parents: *"We do not just teach slides. Your children will physically strip, punch, crimp, and configure these exact cables and controllers in our hands-on labs until they can build these networks with their own hands."*
